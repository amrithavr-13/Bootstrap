FAQ Section

1. How do I report a technical issue or system bug?
You can report technical issues through our support portal by submitting a ticket with detailed information such as error messages, screenshots, device type, and browser or OS version.
2. What systems, browsers, or devices are officially supported?
Our platform supports the latest versions of major browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari) and is compatible with modern desktop and mobile operating systems.
3. How is my data protected when I contact support?
All support communications are secured using encryption, and any data shared is handled in accordance with our privacy and data protection policies.
